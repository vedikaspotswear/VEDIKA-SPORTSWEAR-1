About Us

हमारे यहाँ ग्राहक अपनी पसंद से आइटम प्रिंट करवा सकते हैं — हर डिज़ाइन तैयार किया जा सकता है और पूरी तरह से कस्टमाइज़ किया जा सकता है। हम आपकी सोच को वास्तविकता में बदलने के लिए प्रतिबद्ध हैं!

Vedika Sports Wear is a trusted name in custom sportswear manufacturing. Based in Launa, Jalaun (U.P.), we specialize in designing and producing high-quality sports kits for Kabaddi, Cricket, Football, and more.

We also manufacture school uniforms, including T-shirts and lowers, tailored for comfort and durability. With a focus on quality stitching, modern design, and affordable pricing, Vedika Sports Wear is committed to powering every player — from school teams to tournament champs.
